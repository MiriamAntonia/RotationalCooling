KnifeEdge_OPA.xlxs:
- Data of (manual) knife-edge measurements of the OPA.
- The positions (2.5, 6.5 and 20.5) are measured in inch and refer to the distance between a certain mirror and the knife-edge (as no really better reference point was available).

KnifeEdge_OPA.ipynb:
- Jupyter notebook to extract the beam diameter from the data & get the Gaussian beam parameters from that
